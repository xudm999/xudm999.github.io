What is MGOB?

$MGOB tokens are a BEP-20/ERC-20 governance token for the Mago Hash development. It’s a long-term incentive token that reflects the entity value of Mago Hash. The total supply of $MGOB is permanently fixed and transparent in the blockchain.MAGO holders will be rewarded by staking their tokens, voting for governance, playing the game, and constructing the community. Players will also be able to earn MAGO tokens from user-generated content initiatives.

Economics

Staking

Players could stake their idle tokens in Mago Treasure Pool to receive high $MGOB revenue. The dividends are floating up and down according to the deposit time.

Trading

$MGOB, as Mago’s governance token, can be accepted on the Mago NFT market. Players are free to trade their Magos, land, and limited Mago Badges at different prices.

Governance

Mago Hash DAO is a digital organization that provides a fresh way for investors, game players, and token holders to make relatively fair decisions, making Mago Hash successful.
